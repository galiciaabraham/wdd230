Hierarchy

Job Bank Canada

www.jobbank.gc.ca

The home page of the job bank system from the government of Canada is a great example of visual hierarchy, while it includes elements and a style that are visually appealing it also uses colors, boxes and bold fonts to let you know where are the elements that are important to you. I am looking for a job in Canada as a user support technician and visited this website for that purpose and noticed how good of an example it was of this principle. the box in the middle and the bigger font let you know where is your search menu and options.
