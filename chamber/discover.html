Growth of the Economy

The X - Plaza de la Mexicanidad

This is the most popular and used square in Ciudad Juarez, used to host event, concerts, shows and more. Recently re-named Plaza de la Mexicanidad to exalt the mexican values of hard work and hospitality.

Interactive Museum of La Rodadora

Enjoy the number one interactive museum of the Northern Mexican Border, want to learn more about dinosaurs or wanting to explore the limits of our universe? This is your place!.

The US/Mexican Border

Used by thousands of citizens everyday, the US/Mexican Border stands as a witness of a city that lives in two countries, you will be surprised by the amount of people use this place to simply take a walk, flying kites or ride their bikes and clear out their minds.

Misiones Mall

Welcome to the biggest mall in Ciudad Juarez, the best place for advertising and growing businesses, visited by all the city every weekend you can go watch a movie or have icecream and enjoy the wonders of modern economy.

Samalayuca's Deset Dunes

Feeling adventurous? You may want to visit the dunes of Samalayuca, part of the Chihuahua's desert, one of the most arid places on earth, also home of the home-trucks festivals!

Juarez Central Park

If what you need is a chill night you need to come to the Parque Central, located next to El Museo de la Rodadora it's a great idea to plan on visit both, you can enjoy the best street food in town while you enjoy the local artifical lake and visit the Giraff Modesto, one of the icons of the city.
